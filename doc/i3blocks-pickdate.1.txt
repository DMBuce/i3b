Usage: i3blocks-pickdate

    Shows the date in i3blocks and runs 'pickdate' when clicked on.

Default Config:

    [pickdate]
    command=/usr/bin/i3blocks-pickdate
    BAR_POS=top
    OPTIONS=-c primary
    DISPLAY_FORMAT=%a %Y-%m-%d %H:%M
    FMT=%F
    CAL=cal
    BUTTONS= <:<<: .:>>: >
    SPANATTRS=weight="bold"
    interval=5
